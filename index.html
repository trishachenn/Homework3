About Me!

Hello! My name is Trisha Chennuru and I'm excited to welcome you to my personal website! I'm currently a third year at Northeastern University in Boston and am studying Data Science and Business with a concentration in Marketing. Ever since I first arrived on campus in the fall of 2023, Boston has truly become my second home. One of my favorite parts about living here has been exploring the city’s vibrant and ever-evolving restaurant scene. From cozy cafés in the South End to hidden gems in Allston and waterfront seafood spots in Seaport, Boston offers an endless variety of flavors and experiences that make every meal feel like an adventure. Having grown up in Brookfield, CT there was never many new restaurants to try. Those outings became some of my favorite memories — trying everything from street food to Michelin-starred restaurants, always guided by my mom, who’s the ultimate foodie. Her passion for food definitely rubbed off on me, and now it’s something we continue to bond over, especially when we travel together. I would say that my favorite cuisine is Italian as it's so versatile. This was just further confirmed when I went to Italy for my Sixteenth birthday and got to try all the different pasta, pizza, and seafood options. I hope you enjoy my website, as I am very excited to create it and share more about the food scene in Boston!
